Attila Lőrincz offers a comprehensive suite of civil engineering services tailored to meet the diverse needs of his clients. With a focus on precision, innovation, and sustainability, he delivers exceptional results across various structural projects.

His core services include Structural Analysis, Structural Design, and Static Analysis Documents and Planning. Each service is designed to ensure the highest standards of quality and efficiency, making your project not only structurally sound but also optimized for long-term performance.

Structural Analysis:

This service provides a detailed examination of the physical integrity and behavior of structures under various conditions. Utilizing advanced FEM softwares such as Sofistik, Dlubal, GGU, and RFEM, Attila performs comprehensive simulations and evaluations to predict how structures will respond to environmental forces, loads, and other stressors. This meticulous approach allows him to identify potential issues early in the design process, ensuring that each structure is safe, reliable, and compliant with relevant standards.

Key aspects:

  • In-depth FEM simulations to assess structural performance
  • Load analysis for various types of forces and stress conditions
  • Identification of potential structural weaknesses and failure points
  • Recommendations for reinforcement and optimization

By integrating structural analysis services into your project, you can achieve a deeper understanding of your structure's capabilities and limitations, leading to more informed decision-making and enhanced safety.

Structural Design:

Encompassing the creation of detailed plans and specifications for new constructions and refurbishments. Attila leverages industry-leading CAD tools like Nemetschek and Autocad to produce precise and efficient designs that adhere to all relevant standards, including DIN EN 1990-1998. Whether you're constructing a new railway bridge or renovating an industrial building, Attila’s designs are tailored to meet your specific needs while optimizing material usage and cost efficiency.

Key aspects:

  • Comprehensive design plans for new structures and refurbishments
  • Integration of sustainable and cost-effective materials
  • Adherence to international standards and local building codes
  • Detailed drawings and specifications for construction

With Attila's structural design services, you can ensure that your project is not only aesthetically pleasing but also structurally sound and ready for execution.

Static Analysis & Planning:

Attila provides you with the necessary documentation to validate the structural integrity of your projects with static analysis documents and plans. These documents are crucial for obtaining construction approvals and ensuring that your designs meet all regulatory requirements. He's producing detailed static analysis reports that include cross-sections, plan overviews, and FEM structural models, providing a clear and comprehensive view of your project's structural behavior.

  • Cross-sectional drawings and plan overviews

  • Comprehensive documentation for client and regulatory review

Static analysis documents and planning can streamline your approval process and ensure that your project meets all necessary safety and regulatory standards.

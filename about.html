Biography of Attila

Dipl. Ing. Attila Lörincz is a highly skilled and experienced civil engineer specializing in structural analysis, structural design, and static analysis. With a career spanning over three decades, Attila has made significant contributions to numerous infrastructure projects, particularly in Germany.

  • Early Life & Education

    Attila was born on March 8, 1964, in Klausenburg. He completed his primary and secondary education at the Gymnasium for Physics, Mathematics, and Mechanics, graduating in 1982. Following his school education, he served in the military from 1982 to 1984.
    ‍
    Attila pursued higher education at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca, where he studied civil engineering from 1984 to 1989. He earned his diploma with a thesis on the calculation and design of a prestressed concrete bridge over traffic routes.

  • Professional Experience

    Attila began his career as a planning engineer at UVATERV A.G., where he developed software for static analysis programs. Over the years, he has held various roles, including structural planner, construction supervisor, and project manager. His extensive experience includes working on high-profile projects such as:

    • Stuttgart, 21 Project: Involved in tunnel and infrastructure structural planning, including the design of functional construction pits, diaphragm walls, and pile walls.
    • Bremerhaven, Lock and Steel Bridges: Managed the structural planning and construction supervision for these critical infrastructure projects.
    • Frankfurt Am Main, High-rise Building: Oversaw the static analysis and construction supervision for the redevelopment of a high-rise building.

  • Freelance Career

    Since 2022, Attila has been working as a freelance structural planner and static engineer, focusing on engineering and high-rise buildings, steel construction, and prestressed concrete structures.

    Some of his notable projects are:

    • Railway Bridges and Retaining Structures for DB: Conducted static analysis and design for various railway bridge refurbishments and new constructions.
    • Industrial Pipeline and Steel bridge in Bavaria: Delivered comprehensive structural planning and static analysis for complex engineering structures.
